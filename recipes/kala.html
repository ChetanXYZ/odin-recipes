Kalakand-recipe

Ingredients of Kalakand

  • 20 gms Cashew
  • 20 gram Almonds
  • 20 gram Pistachio
  • 400 gram Paneer
  • 180 gram Full Fat Milk
  • 1 medium spoon Green Cardamom Powder
  • 171 gram Sweet Condensed Milk
  • For Garnish
  • 6 gram Pistachio
  • 10 gram Almonds

How to Make Kalakand

  1. Add Cashews, Almonds, Pistachio and Paneer to the Jar
  2. Grind the ingredients : Blade speed 12 for 10 seconds & Blade speed 11 for 10 secondsAdd the Milk and Green Cardamom Powder
  3. Cook for 15 minutes at 95℃ with the blade speed set to Slow Stir. Add Condensed Milk- Mix in all the ingredients at Speed 1 for 5 seconds
  4. Cook the mixture together for 15 minutes at 95℃ with the blade speed set to Stir

To set the Kalakand

  • Grease a tray or pan with ghee.
  • Top with chopped almonds and pistachio.
  • Pour the Kalakand mixture and refrigerate for 4 hours.

Serving

    Once set, cut into cubes and serve.
